Frequently Asked Questions

Is the platform HIPAA compliant?

Yes, we are 100% HIPAA compliant. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 and SSL/TLS standards.

Can it integrate with our EHR?

Absolutely. We support HL7 and FHIR standards for seamless integration with major EHR systems like Epic, Cerner, and Allscripts.

Do patients need to download an app?

No. Patients can join consultations directly from their web browser on any smartphone, tablet, or computer. Mobile apps are also available for a native experience.

Can we customize the branding?

Yes, we offer white-label options so you can present the platform under your own hospital or clinic brand identity.
